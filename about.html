The Purple Macaroni Confectionery was opened in 2015 by the confectioner Michal Azulai-Granot in Mahane Yehuda market in Jerusalem and quickly became a household name in the Jerusalem confectionery scene. Michal Azulai-Granot returned to Israel after a 10-year stay in France, where she learned the secrets of French confectionery from one of the best confectioners in the world. Upon her return she knew that her purpose was to bring the fragrances of Paris to the Holy city and so it was.
